Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 Cup Floor
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 2 Egg
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • Chocolate Cream
  • Coca powder

Instruction

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. Mix- all ingredients together
  3. Pour batter into pan and bake 30–35 minutes.
  4. Cool the cake, sprinkle chocolate powder on top
  5. Cool the cake, slice, and serve
